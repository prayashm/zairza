About Zairza

Zairza is the technical society of College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar where great ideas combine their efforts to build projects of their dreams. Over the years has been successful in inspiring bright minds into technical aspect of engineering and imparting practical knowledge through support for making various projects. The good thing about Zairza is that it still is young. Young enough to be cool.

Mechatronics

Mechatronics looks after the hardware having tasks of the club. It includes:

  • Robotics
  • SAE
  • Mechanical and Electrical Projects

Software

Well yeah, you can guess! Software covers all the software related projects. It is currently teaching front-end web designing. It also undertakes the responsibility for maintaining sites of some of the other clubs as well!
